What is Project 333?

Project 333 is the minimalist fashion challenge for everyone that invites you to declutter your wardrobe and dress with 33 items or less for 3 months.

What are the rules?

  • When: Every three months
  • What: 33 items including clothing, accessories, jewelry, outerwear, and shoes.
  • What Not: these items are not counted as part of the 33 items-- wedding ring or other sentimental jewelry that you never take off, underwear, sleep wear, in-home lounge wear, and workout clothing (you can only wear your workout clothing to workout)
  • How: Choose 33 items, box up the rest, seal it with tape and put it out of sight.
  • What else: Consider that you are creating a wardrobe that you can live, work and play in for three months. Remember that this is not a project in suffering. If your clothes donâ€™t fit or are in poor condition, replace them.
